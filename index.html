About our initiative

Nowadays if you observe, everyone has a smartphone including the people living on the streets. People who suffer from hunger often will also be having a smartphone and good internet connection. We aim to target these people and to help them during their tough times. For the people who do not have smartphones and suffer the pangs of hunger, we have an option where the donor can contact NGO’s and contribute in eliminating hunger.

To eradicate hunger mainly amongst the people who have smartphones and also the people who do not have a smartphone by tying up with NGO’s and other organization.
